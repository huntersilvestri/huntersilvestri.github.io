A Brief History of Hunter "HCJS" Silvestri

Hunter Silvestri was born in Lake Havasu City, Arizona, on the 30th of May 1997. On no uncertain terms, he should not exist; his parents hated each other so much that his mother moved immediately to the other side of the country as soon as Hunter had fallen out of the womb.

After relocating to Rhode Island as a newborn, Mr. Silvestri grew through an idellic and peaceful childhood at his mother's farm in the middle of a forest of christmas trees. He aspired to be a biologist until his hero Steve Irwin was stabbed through the heart by a sting ray, after which Mr. Silvestri wanted nothing to do with biology and turned wholeheartedly to writing and the visual arts.

After writing his first book and recieving his first kiss in 2011 Mr. Silvestri considered himself finished and quietly retired. He lives in Wales with his wife and dog.
